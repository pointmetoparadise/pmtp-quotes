Sandals Grande St. Lucian

a Caribbean classic on a peninsula in Rodney Bay

A few miles up the road from Secrets, Sandals Grande St. Lucian sits on its own peninsula in Rodney Bay — water on three sides, with the resort straddling a long beach and a calmer lagoon pool. Sandals is couples-only across the brand; no children at any of their resorts.

It's the larger, more activity-rich option: multiple bars and restaurants, watersports across the bay, the Red Lane Spa, and round-trip airport transfers included from either UVF or SLU. Premium spirits are included throughout the property.

Option 3 · Couples-only · 2 rooms · 4 adults

Sandals Regency La Toc

a 220-acre hillside estate above its own crescent beach

About 15 minutes south of Secrets, Sandals Regency La Toc spreads across a 220-acre estate above a half-mile crescent beach in Castries. It's the largest of the three resorts here — more on offer, more variety, more ground to cover. Couples-only, like all Sandals.

What sets it apart is multi-resort access. Sandals St. Lucia guests get full exchange privileges at two sister resorts a short shuttle away — 27 restaurants total across the three properties, which is more dining variety than most all-inclusives offer. Golf at Cap Estate (green fees and transfers) is included, as are PADI scuba certification, Hobie Cats, kayaking, snorkeling, and pickleball. Round-trip UVF transfers come with the room.
